  • High Concept: Your mom is on her way home and you need to clean your room before you get into trouble!
  • Theme: Cleaning
  • Mandated Variety: Timed clicker game. Random spawns of trash.
  • Prototype Goal: Implementing a win/lose condition.
  • Player Experience Goals: A sense of urgency to clean up all of the trash so that they don't lose.
  • Gameplay: There is 50 seconds to click on as much of the trash as possible. There are 160 pieces of trash randomly spawned in the room. If the player does not remove all of the trash on time, they lose.
  • Strategies: Mashing the click button in order to quickly remove as much trash as possible.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: A little vague. You are meant to be a child/young adult living with parents, and you know that if you do not clean up your room before your mom gets home, you will get in trouble.
  • Target Audience: All ages.
  • Play Time: About 50 seconds
