Swamp Man!

  • Originality: I created the level myself with assets downloaded from a source listed below. I used an app called "Tiled" that let me create cutsom levels using tilemaps.
  • Technical Merit: Using Tiled to make a custom level with a tilemap. Spawning in coins when there are none left. Adding bombs each time to increase difficulty.

  • High Concept: Swamp man (you) must collect as many coins as possible while avoiding the bombs that get in his way!
  • Theme: Things must be added as the game progresses, not removed
  • Mandated Variety: 2d platformer. Random bomb spawns. Move with arrow keys. Increase score by collecting coins.
  • Prototype Goal: Custom levels and random spawns
  • Player Experience Goals: Want them to feel cautious as they try to navigate around the increasing bombs to get a better score.
  • Gameplay: The player moves with the arrow keys. Collecting coins will increase score by 1. Once all coins collected, more will spawn and a bomb will be randomly spawned. Hitting bombs will end the game. More bombs are added once all coins collected to increase difficulty.
  • Strategies: At first players will be fast to collect the coins, but will eventually slow down and carefully navigate around the bombs for better scores.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Swamp Man lives in the swamp, and people start to invade his home. Swamp man steals their coins in retaliation, and the invaders are trying to stop him with bombs.
  • Target Audience: All ages
  • Play Time: Anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes.
