  • High Concept: You are a ninja trying to avoid obstacles in your way and survive as long as you can.
  • Theme:
  • Mandated Variety: 2d sidescroller. Spikes and bombs are spawned in at random intervals.
  • Prototype Goal:
  • Player Experience Goals: Focused as they try to jump over spikes and bombs and live as long as possible.
  • Gameplay: The player jumps with the up arrow. Players must jump over the spikes and bombs to avoid being killed. If players hit the spikes or a bombs, the game is over. Score is based on amount of time survived.
  • Strategies: Pressing the up arrow at the correct time to avoid the obstacles.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The ninja is running away from something and must survive the obstacles in his way.
  • Target Audience: All ages
  • Play Time: Anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes.
