Asteroids Attack!

  • Originality: Asteroids are destroyed when you shoot them. There is a time limit to see just how many asteroids you can shoot.
  • Technical Merit: The ship points to wherever the pointer is. The asteroids also spawn offscreen and bounce on an offscreen boundary.

  • High Concept: Shoot as many asteroids as you can within the time limit.
  • Theme: Must be completed in 20 seconds.
  • Mandated Variety: Top-down clicker. User clicks asteroids to gain points. Asteroids are spawned in randomly.
  • Prototype Goal: Time constraints and improving scores.
  • Player Experience Goals: Want them to try to improve their scores each time they play.
  • Gameplay: There are only 20 seconds to click on as many asteroids as you can. The ship will follow the mouse pointer to be a little more immersive. When you click on an asteroid, an explosion sound is played, the asteroid is destroyed, and the score increases by one. The ship cannot move. Once the timer expires, you can no longer click on the asteroids.
  • Strategies: Clicking as fast as you can while also tracking the movements of the asteroids.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Supposed to be abstract. Could be anyone on the ship trying to clear off the asteroids.
  • Target Audience: Kids-adults. Anyone with a desire to beat their top score.
  • Play Time: Exactly 20 seconds.
